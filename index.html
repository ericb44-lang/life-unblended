My Story

I've lived
the unexpected.

I am an emotional health coach specializing in supporting women navigating infidelity and betrayal trauma, narcissistic or emotionally abusive relationships, divorce, single motherhood, remarriage, blended families, online dating and major life reinvention.

My work is deeply personal. I have survived infidelity, divorce, single motherhood, and later remarried, blending a family of nine children. I know firsthand how disorienting it can be to rebuild trust, identity, and stability while still showing up for your children and yourself.

I work with women who are starting over... emotionally, relationally, and professionally. That includes women re-entering the dating world, especially those navigating online dating after divorce or betrayal. I've lived that chapter myself, and I help women approach midlife dating with clarity, confidence, and emotional safety—without fear, urgency, or burnout.

I understand the pressure many women feel to "hold it all together" while silently struggling. I've parented children with ADD, navigated high-conflict co-parenting, and supported women who are exhausted from survival mode and ready for something more grounded and intentional.

My Approach

Practical. Compassionate.
Rooted in real life.

I don't believe in quick fixes, surface level positivity, or asking women to bypass experiences that fundamentally changed them. Healing requires honesty, emotional safety, and a supported path forward.

Our work together focuses on emotional clarity, self-trust, boundaries, and nervous system awareness. We untangle the patterns that keep you stuck, whether in relationships, dating, co-parenting, or self doubt and replace them with skills that support steadiness, discernment, and healthier choices.

This is especially important for women rebuilding after betrayal or navigating relationships in midlife. I bring both lived experience and over a decade of professional work with women into every session.

As a former college athlete, I value resilience and consistency, but I also believe progress comes from alignment, not pressure. My role is not to fix you. It's to help you reconnect with your strength and move forward with grounded confidence.
